This is an example website for the CompTIA PenTest+ Cert Guide (Certification Guide) by Omar Santos and Ron Taylor.

Prepare for the Test!

This book is a complete guide to prepare for the CompTIA PenTest+ certification exam.

What Does It Cover?

It covers all the topics in the exam including plan and scope a security penetration testing (ethical hacking) assessment, understanding legal and compliance requirements, and performing vulnerability scanning and penetration testing using appropriate tools and techniques. In addition, it guides the student on how to analyze the results and write a comprehensive report including remediation techniques and best practices on how to effectively communicate results to the relevant stakeholders.

CompTIA PenTest+ Certification

This book is in complete alignment with the CompTIA PenTest+ certification exam blueprint. The book includes practice questions, as well as real-life scenarios and tips.
